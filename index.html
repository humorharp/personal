Who I Am

I fix operational problems that are costing EMS agencies time, money, and liability exposure.

I spent nearly a decade at Royal Ambulance in Santa Clara County — working my way from EMT to Field Training Officer to Operations Supervisor. Along the way I led formal improvement projects using Lean and DMAIC methodology, built KPI dashboards from scratch, rewrote SOPs, redesigned training programs, and reduced a fleet accident rate by 63% over two years.

I work with EMS organizations, public safety agencies, and healthcare operations teams who need someone who's actually done the job — not just consulted on it from the outside.

THE METHODOLOGY

D
Define
Scope the problem precisely. Establish baseline metrics. Build the business case before touching anything.
M
Measure
Collect data from the actual system — ESO, telematics, forms, reports. No guessing. No assumptions.
A
Analyze
GEMBA walks. Fishbone analysis. Root causes, not symptoms. Data broken into actionable patterns.
I + C
Improve + Control
Deploy targeted countermeasures. Build dashboards that hold the gains after the project ends.
